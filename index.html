Main Menu

The menu has been completely revised and redesigned in the colors of InceptionCloud. Cool animations leave the main menu nice and clear. The direct connect makes it easier for you to join the desired server or world immediately.

Ingame Menu

The in-game menu has also been completely revised and redesigned in the InceptionCloud colors. Again we have added some cool animations. We also removed the "Open to LAN" button and replaced it with the InceptionCloud mod options.

Advanced Tablist

We also made an effort with the tab list. An animation will make it appear cool and smooth on your screen. As a player, you will be marked with a golden apple in the tab list. If you are in a party, this is shown by a cake.
